Data Analysis

Prediction model chosen

We have chosen decision tree to be our prediction model in this project.Decision Tree is a graphical diagram for classification. Each branch of the decision tree presents a potential outcome where the furthest branches on the tree concludes the final answer. Our tree objective is to predict whether the job title same, and to visualize the Decision tree provides industry an easy way to visualize and recognize the possible results of their messy data.Look for openings in the market and reasons for the difficulty in hiring. improve the efficiency and accuracy of job advertisements classification for the Manpower Survey conducted by HQIP of VTC.

Job code

The job code is an identifier used to distinguish different types of jobs, and our model generates a corresponding job code after analyzing the type of job.
