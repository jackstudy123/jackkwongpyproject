Introduction

Project Title

Machine Learning based Desk Research System for Job Advertisement Classification

Background

The Retail Trade Training Board appointed MOV Data Collection Center Limited to assist in conducting the manpower survey of the retail industry in Hong Kong from March to May 2019, for collecting the manpower information and formulating recommendations on future manpower training. Since there were changes on the reference date of this survey and the overall design of the questionnaire, no comparisons with the last one were available in this report. This manpower survey was also supplemented by conducting in-depth interviews with some external industry experts to garner insights into the manpower perspective and training needs of the retail industry.

Project objective

We were going to apply our knowledge that we have learn in class to build a data model that can explain and predict the real situation.

Business requirement

We had to predict the performance of the Retail Trade assess the manpower requirements and training needs of the retail industry, forecast the manpower growth and recommend measures to ease the manpower shortage and sustain talent development.

Factors identified and reasons

  • The definition of job title is vague. Many companies' data do not directly show industry names
  • To enhance the efficiency of the operation efficiency of the Manpower Survey conducted by the HQIP of VTC, including enhancing the data collection, cleaning, modeling and classification job advertisements.
